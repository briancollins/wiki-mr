      <text xml:space="preserve">'''April''' is the fourth month of the year with 30 days. The name April comes from that Latin word ''aperire'' which means &quot;to open&quot;. This probably refers to growing plants in spring. April begins on the same day of week as ''[[July]]'' in all years and also ''[[January]]'' in leap years.

April's flower is the Sweet Pea and its birthstone is the Diamond. The meaning of the Diamond is Innocence.

== April in poetry ==
Poets use ''April'' to mean the end of winter. For example: ''April showers bring [[May]] flowers.''

== Events in April ==
* [[April Fools' Day]] occurs on [[April 1]]st.
* [[Easter]] occurs on a [[week|Sunday]] between [[March 22]]nd and [[April 25]]th.
* Australia and New Zealand celebrate ANZAC Day on 25th April. [http://www.awm.gov.au/dawn/spirit/meaning.asp ANZAC] means - Australian and New Zealand Army Corps and began in 1915.
